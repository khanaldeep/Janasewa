About Us

Welcome to JANASEWA: Transforming waste into wealth. A revolutionary digital platform connecting households, recyclable material sellers, and recycling companies. Empowering individuals to contribute to a sustainable future..

JANASEWA, a user-friendly website, bridges the gap between households, recyclable material sellers, and recycling companies. A centralized platform where sellers can list various materials they no longer need. Middle persons facilitate the transfer of materials from sellers to buyers, ensuring seamless transactions and employment opportunities.

Reduced Landfill Waste Diverting recyclable materials from landfills, minimizing environmental pollution. Conservation of Resources Recycling conserves natural resources, reducing the need for raw material extraction.
